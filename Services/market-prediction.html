Market Price Prediction

Predictive analytics for crop prices to help you maximize profits.

How It Works

Timing the market is key to profitability. Our model analyzes historical market (mandi) prices, current supply levels, import/export trends, and seasonal demand to forecast where prices are heading. This empowers you to decide whether to sell your harvest immediately or store it for a better price.


Key Benefits:

  • Maximize Profit: Sell your crop when the price is at its peak, not when it's convenient.
  • Negotiate Better Prices: Enter negotiations with buyers armed with data on future price trends.
  • Reduce Risk: Avoid price crashes by selling before the market gets flooded.
  • Plan Future Crops: Use long-range forecasts to decide which crops will be most profitable next season.
