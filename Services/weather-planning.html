Weather-Based Planning

Smart farming schedules based on weather forecasts and seasonal patterns.

How It Works

Farming is dictated by the weather. Our planning tool integrates hyper-local, real-time weather forecasts to give you a 7-day operations calendar. It provides alerts and recommendations for optimal times to plant, irrigate, fertilize, or harvest, helping you work *with* the weather, not against it.


Key Benefits:

  • Optimize Timing: Know the perfect window to plant for best germination or to harvest for peak quality.
  • Protect Crops: Receive advance alerts for frost, heavy rain, or extreme heat so you can take protective measures.
  • Efficient Resource Use: Avoid irrigating right before a rainstorm or spraying pesticides when winds are high.
  • Improve Safety: Plan your field operations around safe weather conditions for you and your team.
